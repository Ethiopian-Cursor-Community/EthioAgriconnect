## 📊 AI Scoring Deep Dive

### Activity Score Algorithm (0-100):

**Base Score:** 50 points (neutral)

**Last Active Bonus:**
- Within 7 days: +30 points (highly engaged)
- Within 30 days: +15 points (moderately engaged)
- Over 30 days: -20 points (disengaged)

**Account Age Bonus:**
- Over 90 days: +20 points (established user)
- Over 30 days: +10 points (settled user)
- Under 30 days: 0 points (new user)

**Score Interpretation:**
- **90-100**: Power user (daily engagement, established)
- **70-89**: High activity (weekly engagement)
- **50-69**: Medium activity (bi-weekly engagement)
- **30-49**: Low activity (monthly engagement)
- **0-29**: Inactive (needs immediate attention)

### Risk Level Algorithm:

**Risk Factors:**
- New account (<7 days): +2 points
- No recent activity (>60 days): +1 point
- Suspicious email pattern: +2 points
- Multiple failed login attempts: +1 point
- Unusual behavior patterns: +1 point

**Risk Levels:**
- **Low (0-1 points)**: Normal user, no concerns
- **Medium (2 points)**: Monitor for patterns
- **High (3+ points)**: Immediate review required
