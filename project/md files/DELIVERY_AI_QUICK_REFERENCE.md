# Delivery AI - Quick Reference Guide

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Access the AI Assistant
1. Navigate to any Delivery page (Dashboard or Assigned Deliveries)
2. Click the **"AI Assistant"** button (bottom-right corner)
3. Use Quick Actions or type your question

---

## 🎯 Quick Actions (One-Click)

### 🌡️ Temperature Check
**What it does:** Validates if product temperature is within safe range
**When to use:** Before accepting a pickup from farmer
**Example:** Checks if refrigerated unit temperature is acceptable for the product type

### 🗺️ Route Optimization
**What it does:** Suggests fastest route with traffic considerations
**When to use:** Before starting delivery
**Example:** Provides estimated time, distance, and alternative routes

### 📦 Handling Instructions
**What it does:** Provides product-specific handling procedures
**When to use:** When picking up unfamiliar products
**Example:** Steps for safely loading and transporting the product

### 📋 SOP Retrieval
**What it does:** Instant access to Standard Operating Procedures
**When to use:** When you need to verify company policies
**Example:** Cold-chain management procedures, safety protocols

## ⚡ Pro Tips

### 1. Be Specific
❌ "What about temperature?"
✅ "What temperature for organic chicken?"

### 2. Provide Context
❌ "Route help"
✅ "Best route from Green Valley Farm to 456 Main St?"

### 3. Use Quick Actions First
For common tasks, Quick Actions are faster than typing

### 4. Ask Follow-up Questions
The AI maintains conversation context, so you can ask related questions

### 5. Include Product Details
When asking about handling or temperature, mention the specific product

---

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### AI Not Responding?
- Check internet connection
- Wait a few seconds (AI processing time: 1-3 seconds)
- Try rephrasing your question

### Unclear Response?
- Ask for clarification: "Can you explain that in simpler terms?"
- Be more specific in your question

### Need More Details?
- Ask follow-up questions
- Request step-by-step instructions
- Ask for examples

## 📞 When to Escalate

The AI will tell you when to escalate issues:
- **High severity** compliance violations
- **Customer safety** concerns
- **Legal/regulatory** issues
- **Major delays** or cancellations

Always follow AI escalation recommendations!
