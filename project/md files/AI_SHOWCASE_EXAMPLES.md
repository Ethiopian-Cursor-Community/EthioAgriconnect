# 📋 AI Showcase Page - Example Format Guide

## How Examples Are Displayed

Each feature in the comparison table now includes real-world examples:

### Table Structure

| Feature | Before AI | After AI | Impact |
|---------|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Feature Name** | ✗ Description | ✓ Description | Badge |
| with icon | Red box with example | Green box with example | Color-coded |

---

## Example Formats by Section

### Dashboard Examples

**Revenue Forecasting:**
- ❌ Before: "I made $800 last month, so maybe $850 this month?"
- ✅ After: "AI predicts $1,250 revenue next month with 75% confidence based on seasonal trends"

**Customer Insights:**
- ❌ Before: "I don't know who my best customers are or what they prefer"
- ✅ After: "John Doe is your top customer ($450 spent, 12 orders). He buys vegetables weekly."

### My Products Examples

**Demand Predictions:**
- ❌ Before: "I hope these tomatoes sell... maybe I should stock more?"
- ✅ After: "🟢 High demand - Tomatoes will sell 15 units in next 7 days. Stock up now!"

**Inventory Alerts:**
- ❌ Before: "Oops! I ran out of tomatoes and didn't notice until customers complained"
- ✅ After: "🔴 Alert: Tomatoes low stock (5 units left). Restock soon!"

### Add Product Examples

**AI Descriptions:**
- ❌ Before: Spending 10 minutes writing "Fresh tomatoes. Good quality. Buy now."
- ✅ After: AI writes in 3 seconds: "Premium organic tomatoes, vine-ripened for maximum flavor..."

**Smart Pricing:**
- ❌ Before: "Other farmers charge $3... I'll try $2.80? Or maybe $3.20?"
- ✅ After: "AI suggests $3.50/kg based on: quality (organic), season (high demand), market average"

---

## Visual Design

- **Red boxes** (Before) - Show pain points and frustration
- **Green boxes** (After) - Show AI solutions and success
- **Italic text** - Makes examples stand out from descriptions
- **Border accent** - Left border highlights the example boxes
- **Emojis** - Add visual interest (🟢 🔴 ✓ ✗ 💡)

---

*This format makes the AI improvements concrete, relatable, and compelling for hackathon presentations!*
